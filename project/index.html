Individual Project Site Plan

“Navoo” a Bolivian hardware importing company

Site Name:

Navoo Hardware Imports

This name represents a Bolivian company specializing in importing high-quality hardware products. It is simple, easy to remember, and clearly identifies the nature of the business.

Purpose of the project:

The website will be a three-page informational site for Navoo, a hardware importing business located in La Paz, Bolivia. The site will present the company’s background, the products they import, and contact information for clients.

Scenarios:

The following questions represent the typical inquiries a visitor or potential client might have when visiting Navoo’s website:

  • Which hardware products does Navoo import, and are they available in bulk?
  • How can I contact Navoo to place an order or request a quotation?
